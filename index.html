Who we are

Esaben Communications and Events is an event logistics and marketing company. The company offers A-Z event planning services from a team of experienced and energetic event planners, suppliers, venues and more.
One of the main reasons behind the success of Event Planner is the fact that the team does not charge fees to its clients! With the number of events we organize, Esaben Communications and Events does not need to add exorbitant fees and mark-ups to make its profit margins. This ensures that our clients list, which is constantly growing, make regular use of our services.
